Super Bowl Wins/Loses

This graph shows the number of wins/loses each NFL team has. The Pittsburgh Steelers have the most Super Bowl wins with 6 and the New England Patriots and the Denver Broncos have the most loses with 5. There are 4 teams that aren't listed that haven't made it to a Suber Bowl which are the Detroit Lions, Cleveland Browns, Jacksonville Jaguars, and the Houston Texans.
